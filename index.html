Who I am

Two things,
one person

Analyst and builder. I work on enterprise data and AI systems by day, and ship consumer apps in the time that belongs to me.

I build apps. Small, focused, honest ones. Products that solve one problem well, charge a fair price once, and leave users alone after that. No retention mechanics, no push notification guilt-trips, no freemium tricks.

Alongside that, I work in data and AI — helping organisations figure out how to actually use it rather than just talk about it. Most adoption fails at the human layer, not the technical one.

I write about AI agents, automation, and what it takes to build and distribute software as a solo developer at build-automate-ai-repeat.com.
